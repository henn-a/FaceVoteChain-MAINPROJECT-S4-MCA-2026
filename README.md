# FaceVoteChain-MAINPROJECT-S4-MCA-2026

**Project overview**

**FaceVoteChain** is a secure and transparent voting system designed for college elections, integrating facial recognition and blockchain technology. The system enables students to cast their votes through a digital platform while ensuring strong authentication using real-time facial recognition. During registration, students’ facial data is captured and stored, which is later used to verify their identity before allowing access to the voting system. This eliminates the risks of impersonation and duplicate voting commonly found in traditional voting methods.

In addition to secure authentication, the system uses blockchain technology to store votes in an immutable and tamper-proof manner, ensuring transparency and trust in the election process. The platform provides role-based access for administrators, staff, and students to manage elections, monitor activities, and view results efficiently. By combining biometric verification with secure data storage, FaceVoteChain offers a reliable, scalable, and modern solution that enhances the integrity and efficiency of college elections.

**Objectives**

> To develop a secure voting system for college elections
> To implement facial recognition for real-time voter authentication
> To prevent impersonation and duplicate voting
> To ensure transparency using blockchain-based vote storage
> To maintain data integrity through tamper-proof records
> To provide role-based access for Admin, Staff, and Students
> To enable efficient election management and monitoring
> To create a user-friendly interface for easy voting
> To ensure fast and reliable result generation
> To build a scalable and cost-effective digital voting solution
